SERVICES & ACHIEVEMENT

OUR FEATURES OF EXCELLENCE

AWARD & RECOGNITION

We have so far succeeded in bagging four Regional Shields in 2068, 2070, 2073, 2075 and one national shield in 2074 BS for outstanding scholastic performance

TEAM OF PROFESSIONAL

We hires subject experts and professional trainers to equip teachers with the best tools for effective and most contemporary teaching methods.

AMBIENT INFRASTRUCTURE

We have multi-stored buildings with spacious, well ventilated and bright classrooms. Digitized with latest technology, equipped with science, computer and mathematics labs….

SWIMMING POOL

As a part of extra curriculum activities, Everest will provide swimming classes for its students. For this purpose we have well maintained modern pool that incorporates the latest swimming pool trends, technology and features.

TRANSPORTATION FACILITIES

We transportation own shuttle buses and van to and from all major routes of the city and the suburbs at reasonable fares.

LIBRARY & RESOURCES

We takes pride in its fully loaded library which children can use as a resource centre for the attainment of the pleasure of reading and enhancing reading habits.e

COMPUTER LAB

We have closely acknowledged the importance of computer and IT education in this hi-tech world. Therefore, computer education is an integral part of the school curriculum from grade three to ten.

COUNSELLING SERVICE

Counselling chiefly serves students, teachers, parents and stakeholders with a purpose to meet the various challenges and obstacles arisen in the smooth growth of children.

SCIENCE LAB

We have designed separate labs for physics, chemistry and biology subjects, where practical classes are conducted as per the secondary school curriculum. The labs stand out to be the best of their kind in the entire province

BASKETBALL COURT

We have introduced a fantastic initiative aimed at promoting student engagement and enriching their college experience. The college has launched a basketball program, which not only serves as a platform for self-improvement but also contributes to enhancing extracurricular activities on campus.

CAFETERIA

We have taken a significant stride in enhancing the overall student experience by introducing a cafeteria on college. This cafeteria serves as a crucial addition to the college’s facilities, offering students access to hygienic and nutritious food throughout their academic sessions.

EXTRA CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

We holds pride to conduct Extra curricular activities in various sports and education activities. Our wards are well trained with the professional trainers in respective areas to the best.

NEB Board Toppers

We embodies a pool of talent and epitomizes academic excellence. We inspire curiosity, fostering a culture of lifelong learning among our students. As a result, our passionate students consistently set new records in NEB results year after year. We are immensely proud and celebrate their remarkable achievements!
